About This Website

Some research indicates that it is beneficial for our mental health to record good things in daily life. You may use this website as a good-things-tracker, besides you may connect neighbors to follow what good things happened to their life.

There is no need to strain yourself to record good things as many as possible. Let's start with one, and five is considered enough.

  • Ate tasty food at Starbucks
  • Talked with my friend
  • Played funny video games
  • The waiter at a restaurant I had lunck at smiled at me
  • Had time with family
  • Managed to apply for a new position
  • Woke up early
  • The atmosphere in the early morning was comfortable
